Who We Are

Our club hosts workshops every month to introduce a theme or workshop that the students can base their projects on. Our goal is for students to have a tangible working product that they can be proud of by the end of the meeting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about VikingsDev.

What is VikingsDev

Meetings take place on the third floor of the North Building in Room 315 at Burnaby North Secondary School. Meeting times are now different during quarantine period.

Where can I find VikingsDev?

Find VikingsDev meetings are announced via our discord server. Click Me!

When are meetings?

Meetings take place every Friday after school. They end at 4:30 pm.

Who can participate?

All current students of Burnaby North Secondary School (BNSS) can join VikingsDev! If you're not a student from BNSS, feel free to drop by and check out what we're doing.

Can I play games at VikingsDev?

Sure! We just ask that you go to Room 312 for activities that might distract others.

Do I need to know how to code to be in VikingsDev?

Not at all! Our workshops are super beginner-friendly and we encourage more people to learn how to code. No programming experience required.
