Who We Are

Our club is dedicated to promoting technology, computer science, and programming within youth at Burnaby North Secondary. We hope to make each meeting not only a learning experience, but an enjoyable one.

About

Learn how to code.

Our club creates custom made workshops to help you learn about applicable, real world projects and tools. Our goal is to empower your creativity by providing a platform from where you can build upon and make the workshop your own.

FAQ

What is VikingsDev?

VikingsDev is a club at Burnaby North Secondary, dedicated to inspiring young students to code. We are a part of Hack Club

Where can I find VikingsDev?

Meetings take place on the third floor of the North Building in Room 315 at Burnaby North Secondary School

When are meetings?

Meetings take place every Friday after school. They end at 4:30 pm.

Who can participate?

All current students of Burnaby North Secondary School (BNSS) can join VikingsDev! If you're not a student from BNSS, feel free to drop by and check out what we're doing.

Can I play games at VikingsDev?

Sure! We just ask that you go to Room 312 for activities that might distract others.

Do I need to know how to code to be in VikingsDev?

Not at all! Our workshops are super beginner-friendly and we encourage more people to learn how to code. No programming experience required.
