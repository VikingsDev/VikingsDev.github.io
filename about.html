What We Do


We believe in the next generation of software developers. The coming generation of developers will have access to an unprecedented amount of computing power and will revolutionise the way we live. It is paramount that we inspire a passion for coding in more of our students, and create a generation of technologically informed adults.

Our club hosts mini-hackathons that last for 2 hours every week, Wednesday after school, where we introduce a theme or workshop that the students can base their projects on. Our goal is for students to have a tangible working product that they can be proud of by the end of the meeting.
